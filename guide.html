Searching

There is a search bar on the top-left or top of the map. Input the name of the town/city, or a player (former usernames are supported), and markers will show on the towns/cities found. Clear the search bar to exit.

Show/hide towns

To show/hide the markers of towns, click on the top-right. This might help in reducing some lag.

Airportcalc v2

To launch Airportcalc, click on the top-right.

At the bottom-left, there are three options: Rectangle, Polygon and Circle. Use these to mark out the area of the city/airport.

There are also options to edit the nodes, cut holes, delete and rotate polygons.

At the top of the controls you would see . Select this to indicate that you are marking out the city. Alternatively, select to indicate that you are marking out the airport.

Select to clear all polygons, to export the current measurement as a .apc file, and to import a measurement.

The measurement would be saved if you close and reopen, or reload the page.
